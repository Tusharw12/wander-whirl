Paris, France

Romance, Art & Elegance

Paris, the capital city of France, is a timeless destination known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Walk down the charming streets, admire the Eiffel Tower, or enjoy a croissant at a street café—the charm of Paris is unmatched.

Top Things to Do:

  • Climb the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Stroll through Montmartre
  • Cruise the Seine River at sunset
  • Shop along Champs-Élysées
